The Evidence Loop

  1. 1. PolicyCodify principles, regs, and risk tolerances.
  2. 2. CodeExpress controls as versioned, testable code.
  3. 3. TestAutomated assurance gates in CI/CD.
  4. 4. DeployShip with provenance and signed attestations.
  5. 5. ObserveTelemetry, drift, and incident signals.
  6. 6. EvidenceAuditable artifacts — not promises.

Four Pillars

Governance as Code

Policy, controls, and approvals expressed as code, reviewed in pull requests, enforced by pipelines.

AI Technical Debt Elimination

Find and retire shadow models, undocumented prompts, and untested agents before they compound risk.

Operational Compliance

EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001 — operationalized as continuous controls, not annual audits.

Community-Driven Standards

Open reference implementations, shared evaluation suites, and vendor-neutral interoperability.
